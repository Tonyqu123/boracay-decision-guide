# Hotel Candidate Sources

Last checked: 2026-05-18

This file records why the first seed hotels are allowed into `boracay-hotels.js`.

The app still does not claim these are final recommendations. Before booking, each hotel needs a fresh check on recent reviews, price, cancellation policy, room type, noise, Wi-Fi, and walking distance.

## Maintenance Rule

If a candidate cannot be verified from a current official page, credible booking page, or recent traveler evidence, remove it from the app until it can be checked again.
